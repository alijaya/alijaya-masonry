cols, cell-width, and cell-height

Constants number of cols and item size.

cell-width and cell-height

Derive cols from alijaya-masonry width divided by cell-width.

cols and cell-height

Derive cell-width from alijaya-masonry width divided by number of cols.

cell-width and cell-ratio

Derive cell-height from cell-width divided by cell-ratio.

cell-height and cell-ratio

Derive cell-width from cell-height multiplied by cell-ratio.

cols and cell-ratio

Derive cell-width from alijaya-masonry width divided by number of cols.

Derive cell-height from cell-width divided by cell-ratio.

cell-width, cell-height and cell-ratio

Ignore cell-ratio.

cols, cell-height and cell-ratio

Derive cell-width from cell-height multiplied by cell-ratio.

cols and cell-width

Can't derive cell-height, so the item will take original item height.

row in alijaya-masonry-item become useless.

cell-width only

Can't derive cell-height, so the item will take original item height.

row in alijaya-masonry-item become useless.

cols only

Can't derive cell-height, so the item will take original item height.

row in alijaya-masonry-item become useless.
